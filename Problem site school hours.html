Public schools in America take up around 7 hours.

Problem

Many students must wake up from 5am to 7am so they can be to school on time and must remain there for hours with little sleep. They remain there for hours with little sleep. If school started later or didn’t last as long student would probably have more energy to put into their work improving their grades...

About

Students don’t have as much time to do the things they like and are more likely to be distracted at school because of this.Teachers are stuck at school for even longer than many students and don’t get as much time as they probably like outside of school, they don’t have as much to put into their work

Why This is Important

Student and teachers aren't working to the best of their abilities, also countries with longer school hours have higher raters of student depression and anxiety and have worse scores overall.

Solution

Changing the entire public-school system can’t really be done but learning to manage the time you do have outside of school is one of the best ways to manage this problem
